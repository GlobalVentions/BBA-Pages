The New Trap in the Auto Loan History!

The "yo-yo financing” scheme has taken the world of auto loan by storm. This is happening to all those individuals who wish to buy a car with so-so credit card history. This scheme is being targeted at those individuals who take possession of the new car before completing the financial arrangements. If your financing falls through, you will be pressurized by the dealer to go for a revised plan where you need to pay extra fees – or have to hand over the car to the dealer.

This is not a funny affair. It is quite embarrassing. In fact expensive as well! You have to pay more in order to keep the new car (which you thought, you have already bought) or you end up losing the car.

How one gets caught in the trap!

The “yo-yo” financing scheme, works on the basis that you will get spot delivery of the car without filling up any financial forms. In order words, you get back home in the new car without having to wait for any financial approval. This sounds quite interesting isn’t it? This is where everyone is making the mistake.

In this entire scenario, what actually happens is – you buy the car by filling up some forms and paying a certain amount as down payment. You return home with the new car. A few days later, you receive a call from the dealer informing you that the financing application has failed and that you need to opt for other financing solutions at a higher rate. In case you refuse to do so, you will be asked to hand over the car otherwise they might report it as stolen.

Now you are stuck as you have already informed your friends and relatives about your new car. It becomes a matter of prestige. So you end up opting for the other financing schemes at a higher rate.

State laws vary on this issue:

In some states, you have the option to return the car and get the money invested. However in other states, you are liable to make the complete payment due which gets you stuck in the yo –yo problem.

How to protect yourself from this “yo-yo” scheme?

It is not that difficult to protect yourself from this financial mess – if you know how to deal with this problem.

  • Opt for auto loans before you buy a car: when you have pre-approved financing, you know that every month you will be able to pay the money due. In fact you have a clear idea of how much amount you need to pay and when. This way, you are completely out of the grasp of the dealer.Log in to: www.crowdfundingtools.com , click on their Automotive Calculator and find out how much loan you can apply for.

    Your bank will be more than happy to arrange for the auto loan and you will not be subject to the vulnerable "yo-yo" problem. Even if the dealer offers you better financing plans with interesting rates, you still have a backup plan to fall upon.

  • Always read the contract: Make it a habit to read the contract from cover to cover. In case you fail to understand the financial terms consult a lawyer. Read through the fine print and terms and conditions so that you are in the same page as the dealer.

Bottom Line / Conclusion:

The best way is to shop for your auto loan before you go ahead and make the purchase. This way you know what the different financing options that you have got and what are the prevailing interest rates. Compare the rates and opt for the one that best fits your requirement. This will help you steer clear of the yo-yo financing trap.
