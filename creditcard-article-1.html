Auto loans for people with bad credit

If there is this burning question in anyone’s mind as to whether or not they would qualify for a loan with bad credit then the answer is, yes they would but its going to be a costly affair. Anybody with a bad credit ought to be mentally prepared that they are going to be paying a higher rate than people with good credit. However that shouldn’t be discouraging as there are car loan even for people with bad credit but one ought to be careful while looking for one, as there are possibilities of being overcharged. The art of car financing and securing an easy approval even with bad credit can be broken down into the following steps.

Buying a car with bad credit in 2017

Credit check

First and foremost one ought to find out the root cause of their conviction that one has bad credit or are worried or unsure of their credit scores or maybe someone alluded that one has bad credit. One may think one has bad credit because one could be apprehensive about possibly exceeding one’s budget or perhaps falling behind on payments. Its absolutely important though to be aware of the facts so as to avoid making decisions based on the unknown.

Enhance score

By checking the credit score one would be aware of the areas that need improvement. There could be errors on one’s credit report that could be fixed boosting credit scores. If at all possible, at least a month should be set aside for resolution of disputes on credit reports prior to looking for a car loan. If the credit score is in reality better than what one may have thought then in that case it would be prudent to pay off credit debt, rectify errors or collect on past-due accounts so as to improve credit scores. If the car loan were delayed then that would bring about improvements to credit scores meanwhile.

3. Proper agency

Before you borrow money for buying your car, you need to understand the terms and conditions of the lender thoroughly. When it comes to buying a car, you will find that many financial companies exist in the market. It is only advisable that you avail loan from the bank and not from any other source. The reason behind this is you need to pay a higher rate of interest when you avail loan from a financial company whereas loans given by bank have a lower rate of interest. Lower rate interest means you will not be over-burdened with the monthly payment.

Finding a dealer to work with

If a car is required on demand and the credit score is under the 500 or 600 range for example, even then there are quite a few options to pick and choose from. If the car is going to be purchased through a dealership then one has to be able to find a suitable dealership handling loans of that nature. Its possible to find a dealership like that as dealerships generally have anywhere between 10 and 20 lending institutions that they have tie-ups with and therefore there would be choices with conditions applying meaning the credit status of a borrower. Yet another alternative would be securing the loan from a bank that has built a rapport with the dealership and the dealership reciprocating.

How about pre-approval?

Those with bad credit scores, its recommended that they get pre-approved by a bank or credit union, so that they are better prepared for shopping for a car. Once the loan approval letter has been received car shoppers can visit dealerships for negotiations for better approval. Car loan pre-approval could be likened with mortgage pre-approval as, in both cases one has to submit paperwork as early as possible and knowing the probable obstacles beforehand would prevent any unpleasant surprises later on.

Questions to ask prior to getting a Car Loan

One ought to ensure that a car loan is within one’s budget and therefore the queries that one ought to have in one’s mind are interest rates that are to be paid over the term of the loan, whether there would be any penalties for prepayment and the mandatory steps for refinancing with a higher interest rate.

One ought to be smart when it comes to one’s credit while fishing for an auto loan as a lender pulling a credit report would trigger an inquiry on he borrower’s file and if the inquiries are one too many, that could damage the credit scores. Inquiries on auto loans are considered as a single inquiry by majority of scoring models with a window anywhere between 14 and 45 days. It would therefore be prudent to restrict shopping for auto loan for two weeks so that credit scores aren’t damaged further.

Ways to Save Money on Car Loans

It’s a well-known fact that with poor credit the rate of interest that one would have to pay would be higher until the credit scores are improved. However, there are ways and means of saving money on interest rates even with bad credit.

Selecting a short-term loan like a three-year loan, for instance, typically has a lower interest rate than a five-year loan. Loans for a newer car purchase would be more economical than used car loans but in an one-off case if there is a reasonable offer then one ought to grab it.. Dealers will try and convince unsuspecting customers to opt for pricey add-ons but they will only inflate the sales price.
