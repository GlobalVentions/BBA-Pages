Behind the Score

Get acquainted with the beginning of a great credit history.

Balances

When you have unsettled accounts and/or balances over a given period of time, these debts are reported to credit bureaus by collection agencies and can the negatively impact your credit score.

Learn more

Trade Lines

A trade line is any credit account reporting on your credit report. They can come in many forms, bre it a revolving credit card account or an installment loan. A late payment becomes a negative trade line.

Learn more

Inquiries

When you look to apply for any type of credit, such a a mortgage or auto loan, you are essentially authorizing lenders to ask or "inquire" for a copy of your credit report history from a credit agency.
